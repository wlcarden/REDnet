Once you're in

A few things to set up on your device.

Lock down notifications
Turn off message previews on your lock screen. By default, your phone may show message content in notifications, which anyone holding your phone can read without unlocking it.

iPhone: Settings > Notifications > Element X > Show Previews > Never
Android: Settings > Apps > Element X > Notifications > Sensitive notifications off
Display name
Your display name is visible to everyone in the community. You can change it in Settings > Profile. The same rules apply: do not use your real name, and do not reuse a name from another platform. Your display name and your username do not need to match.
Second device
Log in on a second device if you can. If you lose your phone, a second session (laptop, tablet) lets you get back in without needing the recovery passphrase. Open the same app on another device, log in with your username and password, and verify the new session from your phone.
